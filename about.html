About T Designing & Maintenance

Learn more about our craftsmanship, values, and commitment to quality

Our Story

T Designing & Maintenance was established in 2015 with a passion for creating beautiful, functional spaces through expert carpentry. What started as a small workshop has grown into a trusted name in custom furniture and cabinetry.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional carpentry services that transform spaces and exceed customer expectations. We believe in the beauty of wood and the artistry of craftsmanship.

Why Choose Us?

We specialize in solid wood cupboards and furniture, kitchen cabinets, refurbishing existing kitchen cabinets and more! We aim to provide the best service and take pride in our work by listening to our customers, careful planning, thoughtful execution and dedication to the projects at hand.

We make your vision come to life! T Designing & Maintenance for all your woodworking needs!

Our Values

  • Quality: We use only the finest materials and techniques
  • Precision: Attention to detail in every project
  • Innovation: Creative solutions for unique spaces
  • Reliability: Consistent delivery on our promises
